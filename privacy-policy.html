Privacy Policy

Unless directed by the user, Taplog does not transmit or share any personal information off of your device, including user-specific content in your TapLog database, button labels/names, logged gps coordinates, and user-generated text recorded in logs or input into the app by the user in other ways. Contents of the TapLog database that are shared by users to other services are subject to the privacy policies of those respective services.

TapLog collects and aggregate anonymous usage information to help improve the software. Please contact privacy@taplog.net with any questions, concerns, or feedback.

Explanation of 'Accounts' permission. The 'Accounts' permission on Google Play is currently required for paying subscribers to the backup service. Backup subscribers' email addresses are accessed and stored on the device in private local storage to facilitate backups to Google Drive. Your email address is never transmitted off of the device.
